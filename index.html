Potluck Planner

Plan your potluck with ease

Get Started

Have you ever tried making a Potluck via texting, email, or by sign up sheet. Then you know the struggle of keeping everything organized and accounted for. With Potluck Planner we take the headache out of Potluck Planning. It keeps everything organized into one place so that you and your guests can view the event. See whos accountable for what and have a place to check back for directions and any questions they might have rather than having to call the organizer for information.

Great Features

Create an Event

Create the event, in a few simple steps. Name the event, choose the location, date, and time.

Add Friends

Invite people to your event via your phones text messages, social media and the people receiving can choose to accept or decline the invitation.

Select Food

Choose what food you want to bring and guests will be able to select what foods or drinks they want to be responsible for.
